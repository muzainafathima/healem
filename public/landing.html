How it works

From sign-up to insight in under 2 minutes.

1

Create a free account

Sign up with just your email. Set up your health profile — age, gender, weight, known conditions. This makes every AI output more personalised.

2

Pick a tool

Got a lab report? Upload it. Feeling unwell? Describe your symptoms. Want to know your risk? Take the lifestyle check. Each tool works independently.

3

Get your AI analysis

In seconds, receive a detailed, easy-to-understand health insight — in English or Tamil. Every result includes a clear medical disclaimer and "see a doctor" guidance where needed.

Book a doctor nearby, follow your diet plan, or save your report analysis. All your health data is in one place — synced across devices, not just in your browser.

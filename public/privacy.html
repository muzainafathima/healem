Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

HEAL'EM ("we", "us") is an AI-powered health companion. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and the rights you have under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP) and other applicable laws.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: email address and authentication credentials.
  • Health data (sensitive personal data): symptoms, age, gender, weight, height, lifestyle answers, medical history and uploaded medical reports that you choose to provide.
  • Usage data: appointments you book and features you use.
  • Location: approximate location (only when you use "Find Doctors").

2. How We Use Your Data

We process your health data only with your explicit consent to generate AI-based insights (disease predictions, risk scores, report explanations, diet plans, chatbot responses). Health data is sent to our secure server, which relays it to Google's Gemini API for analysis. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

3. Storage & Security

Account and appointment data are stored in Google Firebase (Firestore). Access is restricted so that you can only read or write your own records. All traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS).

4. Your Rights (DPDP)

  • Access & correction of your personal data.
  • Erasure — delete all your data from within the app (Profile → Delete my account & data).
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Grievance redressal via our Grievance Officer below.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only while your account is active. On deletion, your profile and appointments are permanently removed.

6. Children

HEAL'EM is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly process data of minors.

7. Medical Disclaimer

HEAL'EM provides informational insights only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis or treatment. Always consult a qualified clinician.

8. Grievance Officer

For privacy questions or complaints, contact our Grievance Officer at: privacy@healem.app. We respond within the timelines required by law.
